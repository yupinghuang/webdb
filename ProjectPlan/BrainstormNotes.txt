Get Congressional, Gubernatorial, Presidential election data for each Congressional District in a given year. 

Initial Data:
Year, State, District Num, Voted Democrat, Voted Republican, Voted Independant


GUI:
Type in a Year => Bring up a map of United States for that year =>
Click on state => Bring up Map of Congressional Districts for Given state and year =>
At any one of maps give user the option to retrieve election data.
