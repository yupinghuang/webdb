Instructions

To query the database, select the office, state and the year range and then click "Submit". The results will be displayed on the right. If there is no data matching your request, then no result will be shown. In the table "RepVotes", "DemVotes" and "ThirdVotes" mean respectively the votes that the Democratic party, the Republican party and the third party received. The query results are sorted by county names.
